About Seglat Computer Technologies

Seglat Computer Technologies was founded on the basic concept that by selling Dell and other brand known products directly to customers, we establish a direct line of communication that helps us understand your needs so we can provide the most effective technology solutions available.

Its product line contains more than 50 different items, including Servers, Storages, Desktop and Laptop computers, Printers, Monitors, UPS, Networking Equipments, Software and Consumables.

Seglat Computer Technologies is the leading importer and Distributer of Information Technology Producst and Solutions in Ethopia. Since its inception in 2006, Seglat has developed two branches, and extensive network of markets in major cities supported by a team of qualified technical and sales staff. Teh company currently employes 68 both professional and semi-skilled staff at its Head Office and branch offices.
